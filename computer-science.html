Look for UT Under Construction

iOS App

"Look For UT" is an iOS app that invites new students and visitors to explore UT by solving puzzles with friends. We used Xcode, Swift and Google Firebase to create this multiplayer game. Players are asked to form a group of 5 and stay with their teammates all the time throughout the game. Then, the system will generate 5 puzzles, relating to 5 places on campus, randomly. After they get to all destinations, they will be given a coupon code to the restaurants near campus.

Defend the Throne

Web Game

This is a web-based tower defense game. One cool thing about this game is that the path for the enemies is generated programmatically based on the map. As a result, this game has the ability to allow players create their own maps, and elements, such as waves, dirt path, rocks, houses, etc. would be arranged by the program randomly.
