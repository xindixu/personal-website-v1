Advertising

I am an advertiser! I am exploring this fast-changing and highly-diverse field and creating my own career path in advertising. 

I wasn't sure what I was going to do in the future until I found advertising as my major. I love advertising because it is such a unique field that allows me to merge all different skills and interests I have and just... be myself! To me, it is an attractive world that no one's role here is the same as others.

Right now, I am interning in a small non-profit organization, and in charging all the advertising-related efforts. I love the empowerment the organization given me! Also, I am taking the media planning foundation class at UT Austin. This semester, we are working on a communication strategy RFP for Austin local brand Groovy Automotive.
