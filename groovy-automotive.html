Groovy Automotive - Media Strategy

Project Description

This is a hypothetical media planning project for Austin local brand, Groovy Automotive.

Groovy Automotive has been serving up fun, reliable, and reasonable auto services as the "honest, neighborhood garage" since 1993. Recently, they have been decided to take their story on the road and launch an experimental store in Colorado Springs, CO, a city synonymous with the ideas and persona of "groovy".

Business Objectives

Planning Research & Insights

Just as other professional media agencies, we, Agency6, started with conducting a significant amount of primary and secondary research. This benefits us a lot in terms of understanding our brand, its competitors, as well as local market and potential trade area.

It is the research, that grant us creativity!

Campaign Scope

Market Research

We first distributed tasks among our 6 people. Kaci, Alejandro, and I were responsible for the target market and geographic profile, competitive profile and product & brand variables respectively. These four parts made up our situation analysis.

Then, we all explored on media menu. Finally, Regina put everything together in PowerPoint, and make it neat and stylish.

Product & Brand Variables

I first started with researching on Groovy's website familiarized myself with our brand and product. Then I went to Groovy's Facebook, Yelp, YouTube channel, Groupon, etc for more information. With my research, I came up with three keywords for Groovy: Highest Quality, Friendly, and Local.

Media Menu

Then, only after Kaci settled down our target audience and geographic profile, could I start with our media menu. After a few hours of exploration, I finally learned how to navigate SRDS database. In that database, I found:

  • CPP for local TV and Radio from SQAD
  • Circulation and Ad rates for local newspaper and magazine in our DMA from AAA, with a special focus on ZIP code 80904
  • Display Ad audience metrics & CPM for local & nearby DMA websites from comScore, with a special focus on measuring visitors aged 25-34, 45-54, and 65+.

With Google Adwords, I worked with Regina, first coming up with ideas on various groups of keywords. Then, we entered them into Keyword Planner, set bid to $3.00, and exported data on clicks, impression, cost, CTR, Avg CPC, etc.

On Blue Line Media website, Alejandro and I searched for various OOH media placements, collecting data their potential showings and costs.

Pre-Launch - Mid-May
OOH Billboards
  • Direct offensive: steal business from competitors
  • 2 billboards near ZIP code 80904 by K&S and Steve Mills
  • 600,000 showings, and 90,000 GMPs
Newspaper
  • Build awareness: inform Colorado Springs residents about the new store
  • Direct purchase: provide coupons to people
  • Colorado Springs Independent from Thur to Sat for 10 col” (7 % penetration in ZIP code 80904)
  • The Gazette on Sun for 10 col” (10 % penetration in ZIP code 80904)
  • 164,875 IMPs in Colorado Springs
SEM
  • Generate interactions: increase website visits, inform people who consider a switch or need more information after seeing other ads
  • 20 clicks immediately after the grand opening
  • Estimated CTR 3.08%, 650 IMPs generated from ZIP code 80904
Social Media
  • Foster buzz: invite people to reply and forward our posts for the opening event
  • Give away 50 coupons to 50 random customers
  • Monthly estimated IMP / GPM: 255,161
Launch - June
OOH Billboards (Cont.)
  • Direct offensive: steal business from competitors
  • 2 billboards near ZIP code 80904 by K&S and Steve Mills
OOH Gas Station Nozzles
  • Build brand awareness: inform people about Groovy
  • Generate direct purchase remind people and urge purchase for maintenance services
  • Two gas stations at 27th and West Cucharras Street (closest gas stations to our location)
  • 1,500,000 showings, and 225,000 GMPs
Radio
  • Build brand awareness: spread the news of the grand opening
  • Urge direct purchase: remind people of auto checks or oil changes and encourage them to come by the shop on their way home
  • Higher emphasis on PMD
  • 50% reach * 3 frequency = 150 TRPs
Digital Display Ad
  • Generate direct purchase: urge people to click on it with message “get free coupon”
  • Generate interactions: drive traffic to our website to increase website visit by 15%
  • 9 News, Azteca Colorado & 7 New Denver have about 120,000 A45-54 daily unique visitors
  • Total of 40,000 impression with geo-fences that target at people only from ZIP code 80904
SEM
  • Generate interactions: increase website visits, inform people who consider a switch or need more information after seeing other ads
  • Build brand awareness: get Groovy on top when people search for general terms, like "oil change", "tun up"
  • Total of 100 clicks every Thursdays to Saturdays
  • Estimated CTR 3.08%, 3200 IMPs generated from ZIP code 80904

Monthly estimated IMP / GPM: 357,000
