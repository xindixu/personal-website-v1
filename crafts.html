Crafts

I am a maker!

I officially started my journey as a maker from high school. At that point, I dreamed to become an architect. I started an Architecture Modelling Association in my high school and got my hands dirty on making architecture models.

My latest work related to crafts is Enron Raptor. Our team built 3 raptor suits for stage show Enron at UT Oscar G. Brockett Theatre, from Feb. 20 to March 4, 2018.

Enron Raptor

Theatre Raptor Suits

15 UT students with different backgrounds collaborated with Texas Performing Arts instructors and costume designer, constructed 3 raptor suits for the Spring production of Enron for The University of Texas Department of Theatre and Dance Oscar G. Brockett Theatre.

We drew upon our existing competencies and interests to contribute value and determine technical design solutions, material choices, fabrication schedules, and project milestones.
