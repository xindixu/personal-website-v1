Next Thing You Charge

Project Description

This is a hypothetical advertising campaign for Telsa Motor, aiming to advocate the "Tesla Lifestyle" created by our team, Little Bean Co. "Next Thing You Charge" is our tagline, implying that our revolutionary Telsa Motor is the future, and the future is around the corner. Our electronic car is just another "device" you need to add to your existing devices.

Strategy

Research

As always, we started our project with lots of research, and we finally boiled everything down into this SWOT analysis.  

Brainstorming

Our first meeting to brainstorm was Saturday morning, for real. We sat down, chatted, and had coffee, worked back and forth, to come up with our strategy.

Our goal was to cultivate preference and encourage adoption. Our targets, young, tech-savvy and affluent professional considers that our high-quality technology, Tesla was distant. Thus, we wanted to create a Tesla lifestyle and advocated that.

I love this process so much because it was such a collaborative, creative process while critical thinking and analytical skills were still required. 

Execution

Interactive YouTube Pre-roll Ad

This is an interactive YouTube, pre-roll ad. At first, we ask the viewer to press their spacebar as fast as they could to accelerate this traditional car. But no matter how hard they try; this car moves pretty slow. Then, they are going to plug in the other car, our Tesla Motor. Then, as soon as they press the spacebar, this car will accelerate to desired speed, within a much shorter period. The ad ends with our tagline, and it takes them to our website.

Advantages:

  • Is engaging and fits the brand
  • Gives the clients with countable results and huge data about the potential buyers
